An interesting note about \(\cosh\text{:}\) The graph of \(\cosh\) is the exact shape of a hanging chain. This shape is called a catenary. Contrary to popular belief this is not a parabola. If you invert the graph of \(\cosh\text{,}\) it is also the ideal arch for supporting its weight.
