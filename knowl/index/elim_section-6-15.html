Similarly to row space, the span of columns is called the column space.
\begin{equation*} \text{column space of } \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 4 & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{bmatrix} = \operatorname{span} \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 4 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} , \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 5 \\ 8 \end{bmatrix} , \begin{bmatrix} 3 \\ 6 \\ 9 \end{bmatrix} \right\} . \end{equation*}
