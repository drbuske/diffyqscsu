You saw many differential equations already without perhaps knowing about it. And you even solved simple differential equations when you took calculus. Let us see an example you may not have seen:
\begin{equation} \frac{dx}{dt} + x = 2 \cos t .\tag{1} \end{equation}
Here \(x\) is the dependent variable and \(t\) is the independent variable. Equation (1) is a basic example of a differential equation. It is an example of a first order differential equation, since it involves only the first derivative of the dependent variable.
