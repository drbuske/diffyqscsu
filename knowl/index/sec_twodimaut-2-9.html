Case 2. Suppose both eigenvalues are negative. For example, take the negation of the matrix in case 1, \(\left[ \begin{smallmatrix} -1 & -1 \\ 0 & -2 \end{smallmatrix} \right]\text{.}\) The eigenvalues are \(-1\) and \(-2\) and corresponding eigenvectors are the same, \(\left[ \begin{smallmatrix} 1 \\ 0 \end{smallmatrix} \right]\) and \(\left[ \begin{smallmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{smallmatrix} \right]\text{.}\) The calculation and the picture are almost the same.
