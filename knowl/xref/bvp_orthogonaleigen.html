Theorem 5.1.1.

Suppose that \(x_1(t)\) and \(x_2(t)\) are two eigenfunctions of the problem (5.1), (5.2) or (5.3) for two different eigenvalues \(\lambda_1\) and \(\lambda_2\text{.}\) Then they are orthogonal in the sense that
\begin{equation*} \int_a^b x_1(t) x_2(t) \,dt = 0 . \end{equation*}
