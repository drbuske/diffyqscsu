Exercise 5.9.4.

Let \(R\) be the region described by \(0 < x < \pi\) and \(0 < y < \pi\text{.}\) Solve
\begin{equation*} \Delta u = 0, \quad u(x,0) = 0, \quad u(x,\pi) = \pi, \quad u(0,y) = y, \quad u(\pi,y) = y . \end{equation*}
Hint: Try a solution of the form \(u(x,y) = X(x) + Y(y)\) (different separation of variables).
